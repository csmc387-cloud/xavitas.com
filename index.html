About the Conference

Explore. Engage. Excel.

We are proud to present the second edition of Xavitas MUN — hosted by St. Xavier's High School, Gurgaon, and powered by Scholarium Foundation. This is a premier Model United Nations conference designed to challenge young minds and foster global diplomatic thinking among students of Grades VI through XII.

Delegates engage in rigorous committee sessions, draft resolutions, and embody the spirit of international cooperation across nine diverse committees spanning global governance, social justice, intelligence, motorsport, and parliamentary debate.

Guided by our motto — Explore. Engage. Excel. — Xavitas MUN is more than a simulation; it is a movement. Whether you're a seasoned delegate or stepping onto the floor for the first time, this is your stage.

At the heart of Xavitas is the phoenix — fierce, radiant, wings outstretched — symbolizing the indomitable human spirit. Like the phoenix, we rise renewed in thought and purpose after every challenge.

Scholarium Foundation is a youth-focused educational initiative committed to building the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and change-makers. Founded on the belief that education is more than instruction — it is inspiration — Scholarium creates platforms where young voices are amplified and potential is fully unlocked.

As the powering institution behind Xavitas MUN, Scholarium brings organizational excellence, institutional expertise, and an unwavering passion for student development. Every conference and competition under Scholarium's umbrella is designed to challenge, inspire, and transform.

Under the leadership of Founder CEO Mr. Waleed Ahmar, Scholarium has grown into a trusted name in student-led conference management across the National Capital Region, partnering with leading schools to deliver world-class MUN experiences.
